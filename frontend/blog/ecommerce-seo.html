E-commerce SEO Best Practices

Drive more sales with these SEO strategies for online stores.

E-commerce SEO requires special attention to product pages and category structure. Best practices include:

Product Optimization

Optimize product titles and descriptions with unique, keyword-rich content.

Schema Markup

Use Product schema markup for rich snippets in search results.

Category Pages

Create unique, descriptive category pages with proper internal linking.

Breadcrumb Navigation

Implement breadcrumb navigation for better user experience and SEO.

Image Optimization

Optimize product images with alt text, descriptive filenames, and compression.

Out-of-Stock Handling

Properly handle out-of-stock items without losing SEO value.

Internal Linking

Build strategic internal links between related products and categories.
